# Chimera — Session Progress

## Project
CS2 VLM research. Two-stage training: SFT (vision) → GRPO (reasoning) for Qwen3.5-35B-A3B MoE.
- SFT: teach model to read the HUD — synchronize perceived game state with demo ground truth
- GRPO: teach model strategic reasoning — decisions scored against pro play + round outcomes

## Key design decisions
- SFT ground truth: demo-to-VOD synchronization, not model labeling
- GRPO reward asymmetry: deviating from a winning pro play is NOT penalized
  hard (model's alternative might work), but endorsing a losing play is
- Reward architecture simplified (2026-03-25): 2 signals — R_percept + R_strategy
  R_decision and R_outcome are ablation baselines only
  Ω = a·w + (1-a)·(1-w) — clean, no magic numbers
- RECALL: kNN retrieval over tactical embeddings for counterfactual advantage
- Model: Qwen3.5-35B-A3B MoE bf16 on H200 SXM (D022)
- Data sparsity (D023): L0 contrastive pairing marginal at 4 demos,
  L1 needs 100+ demos. GRPO can proceed with positive signal only.
