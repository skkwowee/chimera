# Chimera — First-Principles Plan
*Synthesized 2026-07-18 from the Anthropic-lineage brief, ten interrogated + red-teamed commitments, `claude-progress.txt` (Methodology Reset v2 runbook), and the docs cited therein. Rule enforced throughout: no step is justified by "that's what we built." Path-dependence, where found, is marked ⚠ and named.*

---

## 1. The Causal Chain

The whole project is one argument. Each numbered step exists because the previous one forces it; where a step does **not** follow necessarily, it is flagged ⚠ with the honest reason it's there.

**Fact 0 (the domain).** Competitive CS2 is a round-structured, zero-sum, partial-information game. Its tactical content is conditional structure on a handful of state variables — bomb state, round clock, economy, who-is-alive-where — and its futures are genuinely multimodal at decision points (push/hold/rotate), because the deciding variables (comms, calls, intent) are not in any observable state and equilibrium play actively randomizes. Pro skill is behaviorally *anticipation under information asymmetry*.

**Step 1 — Data: parsed pro demos, not pixels, not self-play.** CS2 has no fast headless simulator (unlike Go/SC2), so interaction-based world models (MuZero/Dreamer-style) are off the table; the model must be observational. Demos deliver complete privileged symbolic state at zero perception cost — pixels would spend the whole budget re-solving perception VPT needed 70k hours for, and would recover a *worse* (single-POV) version of what the parser emits exactly. Pro tier is forced because the model learns engine∘demonstrator-policy, and every downstream stage samples futures from that behavioral prior — a low-tier prior would grade "reasoning" against bad play. ⚠ Not forced: the corpus **size** (~92 matches — defended by a saturation number measured under the abandoned sparse objective; the dense-objective scaling curve has never been run), the **8Hz cadence** (inherited from the dead round-encoder era; 1–2 frames per peeker-advantage window, unablated), and the exact **56-d player block** (parser-default; active smokes/mollies missing despite being perception primitives by the project's own rule).

**Step 2 — Objective: dense next-state prediction.** Given perfect state and zero labels, supervision must be self-generated, and the only dense, non-circular, unfakeable self-supervision is the future itself. This is measured, not asserted: outcome supervision is ~1 bit/round and saturated the old round encoder at ~16 demos (0.775 ceiling); caption supervision was circular (+0.008). Events-as-inputs would presuppose the tactical abstractions the thesis wants to *emerge* as prediction surprise. Rounds are the game's own reset unit → round-as-document attention; matches are the data's correlation length → match-level split.

**Step 3 — Target: a distribution over displacement, not a point.** Because the branch variable is hidden and strategically noised (Fact 0), P(future | observable history) has irreducible entropy concentrated on well-separated modes; an L2 head converges to the conditional mean — a ghost-step no player takes, sometimes inside a wall (measured: regression jitter 28–32u vs copy 3u; kill criterion fired). Position/where is the right field because most of the round, spatial reallocation of 10 agents *is* the tactic. Categorical-over-codebook + refine specifically, because three consumers require it: exact per-frame NLL (surprise→events), enumerable support (GeoGate legality masking), one-shot sampling (GRPO groups). ⚠ Not forced: the hand-designed 6×16 polar geometry of the 97 classes (round numbers, horizon-fragile — 32/97 dead at k=4; field practice is k-means anchors), and the never-run GMM comparison the design doc itself promised.

**Step 4 — Horizon: rollout-native short steps.** Motion is momentum-trivial <2s and decision-dominated >4s (res/d crosses 1.0); tactical horizons need 5–10s. The honest load-bearing argument for reaching them by sampled 500ms autoregressive rollout is **downstream consumption**: GRPO's reward is value read on *intermediate* imagined frames (round value is path-dependent — deaths are absorbing, ordering matters), the bridge verbalizes *lines*, and 10 coupled agents stay jointly coherent only under stepwise re-simulation. ⚠ The docs headline the wrong reason: "direct long-k heads mode-collapse" is false for multimodal anchor heads (MultiPath/MTR predict 6–8s directly and own the minADE-K metric Chimera adopted); that control was never run. ⚠ Scheduled sampling specifically is a budget choice (1.27× vs 7.5× on one 4090), not a theorem — correctly pre-registered with an SS-off control and a negative-result clause. Acceptance is coverage (minADE-K) because a set-valued claim is the only honest claim type here — but the coverage number is currently unfalsifiable against pure variance until the fair stochastic baseline (E3) lands.

**Step 5 — Value: P(CT win) as a *detached* readout.** The thesis (C1) is that prediction alone yields strategic abstraction, operationalized as decodability of round outcome from the frozen trunk. That measurement is void if outcome gradients (or value-AUC checkpoint selection — the deleted best.pt leak) touch the trunk: you'd inject the answer, then discover it (the project's own confirmed finding F2). Stop-grad + unit test makes exogeneity machine-checkable. Round-win is the unique label that is terminal (the game scores rounds, nothing else), objective (in the demo file), annotation-free. ⚠ The in-training detached head is convenience ("code was there") — the reported number is the post-hoc probe regardless; and the label was historically inherited from Era-1's tensors, with the first-principles defense constructed after.

**Step 6 — Measurement: frozen linear probes vs floor and ceiling, never prediction loss.** Loss is a scalar over marginal dynamics and cannot distinguish better physics from better tactics (the +27.2pp facing-shortcut proves loss and understanding dissociate here). Presence of information is vacuous (raw state determines everything); the question is *accessibility*, measurable only by a fixed-minimal-capacity readout — linear, per SSL and interpretability convention. "Breaks the label-starvation ceiling" is only falsifiable against a measured ceiling under one identical protocol. ⚠ The current ceiling (legacy round encoder) is path-dependent and apples-to-oranges (different metric, probe class, val set — T1); the keystone comparison has literally never been run (F0). The instrument is right; the instantiation isn't yet.

**Step 7 — Generalization: one held-out map.** On the training distribution, "learned the rules" and "memorized six layouts" are observationally equivalent; only withholding an environment separates them (the ProcGen lesson). CS2's rulebook genuinely factorizes from its maps (economy, timer, trade windows), so a transferable component provably exists — the holdout is falsifiable, not unfair. The map axis is the only cut this corpus can make while holding teams/meta fixed (303/367 OOD rounds share series with train). ⚠ Global /3000 coordinates fingerprint maps, downgrading the claim to "data-transfer + label-efficiency," not layout-invariance; the coordinate frame is defended partly because changing it would unlock Knob 1 — though displacements are origin-invariant, so translation-only egocentric coords would *not* actually break the dist head. ⚠ n=1 and overpass-specifically were co-chosen for cost.

**Step 8 — Verbalization: frozen latent → frozen LLM through a small trainable bridge.** The thesis needs tactical language, and language provably cannot *create* understanding from clean symbolic state (+0.008); it can only *translate* a forward model's foresight. A translation claim is falsifiable only if the translated object is fixed → frozen world model as measurement instrument (principled). Frozen Qwen + QLoRA ⚠ is budget-forced (one 80GB pod, solo researcher) — with the real side-benefit that a frozen base anchors output to readable text. ⚠ Soft tokens over head-readout/tool-calling currently survive on one unproven hypothesis — that the per-token [11,512] grid holds head-inaccessible structure — while the pooled-512 probe (R²=0.92 from head-output text) is affirmative evidence for the simpler design; and the "differentiability for GRPO" defense is void (GRPO never backprops through conditioning). ⚠ M=32/Perceiver is BLIP-2/Flamingo convention, not derived. ⚠ The final-layer bridge tap is untested path-dependence ("it's what the value head reads"); the workspace paper's middle-layer finding gives concrete reason to sweep layers 3–6 first.

**Step 9 — Faithfulness: the NLA-style gate.** In CS2, plausible tactical talk is cheap (genre clichés) and state-specific foresight is expensive — so fluent text can carry zero bits about *this* latent, and Phase-3 RL would amplify exactly that. Firewalled reconstruction is the only label-free lower bound on latent-specific information in free-form text (decodability ⇒ I(text; z) > 0); ablation tests *use*, recon tests *rendering* — orthogonal, both required. Chimera's inversion of NLA — reported decoder trained separately on frozen outputs, recon as constraint never summed reward — is the correct adaptation: evaluator and optimizee must not share gradients when the output is a certificate. ⚠ The default recon target (pooled-512) was chosen because value_head already consumed it — and the project's own probe shows it's too easy (6 effective dims) to fail. The principled target is the head-Jacobian (decision-relevant) subspace — currently fallback #2, should be primary. ⚠ As implemented, the gate is broken in code (B1 gold-leak, B2 value-agreement TODO, B3 post-EOS bypass). Hard ceiling, owned in the paper: recon certifies information *present*, never reasons *meant or used* — which is why ablation, a fact audit, and counterfactual flip-tests must stay co-equal.

**Step 10 — RL: GRPO with grounded rewards.** SFT can only raise likelihood of text you have; no non-circular corpus of good CS2 reasoning exists (templates are scaffolding, commentary misaligns ~25%/event, Claude is a prior). Improving *truthfulness of novel sampled statements* requires reward against an external checker. In a spectator corpus with no environment, every candidate reward is model-authored (value head, judge, preference head — all circular or Goodhart-prone, with F08v4's reward collapse as in-house proof) **except one**: the realized demo future. Demos give complete future state, so verbalized predictions have deterministic truth values — one of the rare domains where RLVR's verifiable checker exists for open tactical language. The 16 sampled rollouts are the group; KL anchors to the SFT bridge; recon-τ filters advantages. ⚠ **The repo has not committed to this reward**: bridge-design §5 and the ledger still spec value-through-rollout (self-referential) as the sole signal — the grounded version exists only in a pivot header. ⚠ GRPO-the-optimizer over ReST/DPO is a defensible RLVR-era default plus `train_grpo.py` surviving from the parked VLM era — partial path-dependence, admitted. ⚠ "Reasoning := forecasting" is forced by the data regime (no action-conditioning ⇒ no verifiable counterfactuals), not by the game; the paper must scope the claim accordingly.

**The spine, compressed:** no simulator → demos → learns engine∘pro-policy → only dense non-circular signal is the future → futures are multimodal → distributional head → tactics live at 5–10s and value is path-dependent → sampled rollouts → thesis needs proof of abstraction → detached value + frozen probes vs a measured ceiling → rule-vs-layout needs an intervention → map holdout → thesis needs language → translation of a frozen instrument → translation needs a certificate → NLA gate → certificate must survive optimization → grounded GRPO. Every ⚠ above is a place where a *parameter* of a forced step was inherited rather than derived — the steps themselves survive.

## 2. Per-Step Cards

### Card 1 — Corpus
**What:** Pro HLTV demos → 8Hz tensors (11 tokens/frame), round-scoped, match-split, team-canonicalized dedup.
**Bedrock:** Observational learner learns engine∘policy over channels the corpus carries → demonstrator must be worth modeling; channels must include the game's decision variables. Sparse targets measurably starve (~1 bit/round, saturation at 16 demos). Split unit must dominate correlation length → match-level.
**CS2:** Rounds = hard-reset episodes; bomb plant flips the objective; clock forces aggression; corpus defects (dead bomb bits, pause clocks, freeze frames) each delete a thesis-critical conditioning variable at a decision margin (~0.02 AUC) where noise *is* the threshold.
**Lineage:** LM dense-pretraining bet; AlphaStar/OpenAI-Five structured-state factoring; MLMove token layout; sports-ML split hygiene.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Action-conditioned augmentation (pseudo-action tokens) — trigger: value-head AUC degrades sharply on off-modal rollouts. Scale-out — trigger: dense-objective scaling curve still improving at 100% of matches.
**Weakest link:** 8Hz inherited (TODO'd in the datasheet); size defended by an old-era measurement; smoke walls invisible.

### Card 2 — Next-State Objective
**What:** 19M causal transformer; 12s history → 500ms-ahead per-player displacement distribution + state residuals; no labels, no outcome in the representation loop.
**Bedrock:** Every supervised target is starved (1 bit/round) or circular (+0.008 captions); the future is dense, self-labeled, unfakeable-by-paraphrase. Tactics = the predictive information about motion that survives after momentum (res/d crosses 1.0 at ~2s, measured).
**CS2:** ~960 frames/round vs 1 outcome bit; pro motion is engine∘policy driven by inferable-but-unobserved intent; 12s covers the evidence (util throw, death, peek).
**Lineage:** LM bet on game frames; MuZero/Dreamer heads-on-latent contrast; IRIS/Genie discrete futures; BC rejected for lack of an honest action vocabulary.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Masked/contrastive SSL — trigger: probe gate fails the 0.775 ceiling (then generation bought nothing representationally; run it as the fallback arm).
**Weakest link:** Strong claim already fell (copy beats argmax on turns); the decisive pre-registered probe-transfer test has **not yet been run**; the exact config (k, window, target field) is a survivor of local kill-tests, not a sweep.

### Card 3 — Distributional Head
**What:** 97-class (1 + 6 rings × 16 sectors) classify-then-refine displacement head; sample or argmax + learned offset.
**Bedrock:** Hidden intent + adversarial randomization ⇒ irreducible multimodal entropy; L2's target statistic lies off the support (measured jitter/kill-criterion). Categorical uniquely gives exact NLL (surprise), maskable support (GeoGate), cheap sampling (GRPO) simultaneously.
**CS2:** 39.4% of alive frames are <8u — holding angles is a discrete tactical action deserving a point mass; speeds are engine-quantized; fakes exist because opponents model your movement distribution.
**Lineage:** MultiPath/MTR anchor-classify-then-refine; BeT bin+offset; C51/"Stop Regressing"; MDN rejected (collapse pathologies), DIAMOND-style diffusion rejected (no exact NLL, sampling cost).
**Best alternative + trigger:** k-means-derived codebook (drop-in, same loss) — trigger: another manual edge-refit cycle or occupancy-gate failure. GMM 1-day arm — trigger: GeoGate veto-rate logging shows exact masking isn't load-bearing.
**Weakest link:** 6 and 16 are unablated round numbers (32/97 dead at k=4, teleport hazard); GMM comparison promised, never run; coverage win currently unfalsifiable vs any dispersed sampler (E3).

### Card 4 — Rollout-Native
**What:** One 500ms model; horizons {1,2,5,7,10}s via K sampled AR rollouts; scheduled-sampling training; minADE-K acceptance.
**Bedrock:** Round value is path-dependent (absorbing deaths, event ordering) ⇒ reward/verbalization consume intermediate frames only rollouts produce; 10-agent joint coherence needs stepwise re-simulation; AR self-composition ⇒ covariate shift (Ross & Bagnell) ⇒ training must see self-generated states *in some form*.
**CS2:** <2s strafe physics, 3–5s executes, 5–15s rotations; the mean of "commit A"/"rotate B" is inside a wall; K-rollout set = the GRPO group.
**Lineage:** minADE-K from Argoverse/WOMD; SS from Bengio 2015 (Huszár's improperness acknowledged via the SS-off control); Dreamer imagination; in-house cv-residual necropsy (single-step win, 2.5× closed-loop blowup).
**Best alternative + trigger:** Direct K-hypothesis joint decoder — trigger: pilot direct head at t+{40,80} matches rolled coverage; or Phase-3 converges on terminal-only rewards (the pro-rollout argument evaporates).
**Weakest link:** Headlined rationale (mode collapse of direct heads) is false for anchor heads; SS chosen by a 1.27×-vs-7.5× budget fact; the coverage claim is a design intention until E3 baseline + R5 harness exist.

### Card 5 — Detached Value Head
**What:** Trunk trained by prediction only; P(CT win) head behind stop-grad, end-phase-masked; unit test proves zero outcome gradient; best.pt (value-selected) abolished.
**Bedrock:** A probe licenses a content claim only if its target is exogenous to representation-making; co-training (confirmed F2) and value-based checkpoint selection both inject the answer. Stop-grad makes exogeneity machine-checkable — the canonical model *is* the vw=0 control.
**CS2:** The game scores rounds, only rounds — a 1v4 clutch and a 5v0 are one point each; round_winner is in the demo file (annotation-free, objective); post-round frames leak the winner → end-phase mask.
**Lineage:** Alain & Bengio read-only probes; Belinkov pitfalls; deliberate inversion of MuZero (value as evidence, not planning input); co-trained twin retained as labeled engineering arm.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Co-trained twin for the GRPO product line — trigger: detached probe fails baselines (pre-registered contingency) or twin beats it by ΔAUC > 0.05.
**Weakest link:** Entirely aspirational — the detached model has never been trained; every headline value number comes from the repudiated regime. Architecture/hparams were tuned while value curves were visible — a selection channel no unit test closes; disclose, bound with the twin.

### Card 6 — Frozen-Probe Judgment
**What:** Freeze latent → linear probes (value, events) vs raw-feature floor and supervised ceiling; prediction loss never accepted as evidence.
**Bedrock:** O(1) supervision bit vs O(10⁵) observed values per round forces the dense objective; loss is unattributable (physics vs tactics); presence is vacuous — accessibility needs a fixed-minimal-capacity readout; "breaks the ceiling" is only testable against a measured ceiling under one protocol.
**CS2:** 8Hz stream is kinematically dominated; decision-relevant quantities (value, events) are what casters/coaches reason in; awpy events are outcome-timed, so causal-latent probes test anticipation.
**Lineage:** Alain & Bengio; Hewitt-Liang control tasks; SimCLR linear-eval; MuZero's refusal of observation fidelity; Anthropic linear-representation stance.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Absorb, don't replace: label-efficiency curves + matched-MLP-with-control-task insurance; matched-capacity supervised transformer as ceiling #2 — trigger: it beats the retrained round encoder (then the old ceiling was a strawman).
**Weakest link:** Keystone comparison never run (F0) and currently apples-to-oranges (T1); ceiling is path-dependent; existing value numbers end-phase-contaminated (O3).

### Card 7 — OOD Map Holdout
**What:** de_overpass (367 rounds) fully held out; /3000 global coords kept; zeroed map one-hot at OOD decode, with an ID-zeroed control and map-dropout fallback.
**Bedrock:** Rule-learning vs layout-memorization are observationally equivalent in-distribution; only an intervention separates them; CS2's rulebook (economy, timer, trades, man-count) factorizes from geometry, so a transferable component provably exists — falsifiable, not unfair.
**CS2:** Overpass is planar (avoids the nuke verticality confound); pro series put the same teams on sibling maps → the only confound-controlled cut this corpus affords; claims scoped to data-transfer + label-efficiency, not identity-invariance.
**Lineage:** CoinRun/ProcGen held-out-environment protocol; frozen-probe label-efficiency framing; the anubis zero-one-hot scar motivating the control.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Egocentric input frame — trigger (softened): OOD coverage skill <0 at horizons ≥2s while res/d matches ID band and a 1k-step fine-tune recovers. Second holdout arms (~$20) — trigger: any reviewer demand, or just buy it.
**Weakest link:** /3000 retention is part process-lock (displacements are origin-invariant — the Knob-1 defense is overstated); n=1 and overpass co-chosen for cost; deliverable is weaker than the README's thesis sentence.

### Card 8 — Language Bridge
**What:** Frozen 19M latent → trainable featurizer + 32-query Perceiver → soft tokens into frozen Qwen-35B + QLoRA; gated by ablate-the-latent and recon.
**Bedrock:** Language can't create understanding from clean symbolic state (+0.008) — only translate foresight that lives in the forward model; translation claims are falsifiable only against a frozen instrument; tactical content is low-dimensional (casters compress a round to two sentences), so a small bridge is feasible in principle.
**CS2:** Multimodal futures are the non-circular grounding content — text that's a function of the current frame is exactly what ablation punishes; 11-token factoring mirrors the game's ontology.
**Lineage:** Frozen → Flamingo → BLIP-2 → LLaVA → QLoRA; NLA for the gate; predictive-head channels appended (no VLM lineage has this).
**Best alternative + trigger:** Head-readout/tool-calling — trigger: grid-target capacity probe shows head-output text reconstructs [11,512] within ~0.05 FVE of the latent itself, or SAE audit finds no head-inaccessible feature, or ablate stays ≈0 after escalations. 4B-first hybrid — trigger: 35B gate run fails untriageably or pod slips >2 weeks.
**Weakest link:** Soft tokens rest entirely on the unrun grid hypothesis; pooled-512 evidence points the other way; frozen-35B is budget-forced; the final-layer tap is unswept.

### Card 9 — NLA Faithfulness Gate
**What:** Firewalled text-only reconstructor must rebuild the latent from Qwen's string, vs shuffled/ablated/mean controls, plus value-head-agreement, readability, and ablate; constraint-filter in GRPO, never summed reward.
**Bedrock:** Plausible CS2 talk is cheap, state-specific foresight expensive; RL amplifies whatever passes reward → certify before optimizing. Firewalled decodability is the only label-free lower bound on I(text; z). Hard ceiling: certifies presence, never meaning/mechanism → must remain a conjunction with causal legs.
**CS2:** Decision-relevant frame content is ~6–8 effective dims — the game's compressibility is what makes a sentence-length carrier feasible; the gate must weight foresight channels over scoreboard-derivable facts.
**Lineage:** Anthropic NLA (verbalizer/reconstructor, FVE, confabulation + degenerate-code failures), inverted: separate frozen-output decoder (no shared gradients — certificate, not readout); J-space paper justifying a head-relevant target; CycleGAN steganography lesson.
**Best alternative + trigger:** Reweighted conjunction, not replacement: exact-match fact audit (replaces the B2 TODO now) + counterfactual flip-test (edit state, demand text flips) — trigger: falsified-text probe shows recon insensitive to semantics, or both principled targets land outside the useful band.
**Weakest link:** Default target chosen by convenience and shown too easy; gate broken in code (B1/B2/B3); "no answer key exists" is overstated — tick data is one for factual claims.

### Card 10 — Grounded GRPO
**What:** Policy verbalizes predictions; 16 world-model rollouts = the group; each completion scored against the *actual* demo future; group-relative advantages; KL to SFT bridge; recon-τ filter; no human labels.
**Bedrock:** SFT can't reward truth of novel samples; no non-circular demonstration corpus exists; in a no-environment regime the realized future is the *unique* non-model-authored scalar — the only reward whose optimum is reality, not a prior (Gao et al. overoptimization; F08v4's in-house collapse).
**CS2:** Complete privileged future state at 8Hz gives deterministic truth values for "A execute in ~3s / CT4 rotates / CT wins" — a rare RLVR-checkable open-language domain; anticipation is constitutive of pro skill, so forecast accuracy isn't a proxy.
**Lineage:** GRPO (Shao 2024); RLVR (R1, Tülu 3); InstructGPT KL; Dreamer imagination repurposed as group *generator*, expressly not reward.
**Best alternative + trigger:** One ReST/rejection-sampling round or offline margin-filtered DPO first — trigger: checker AUC <0.75 on pseudo-gold, reward ICC <0.2, or an F08v4-repeat spread collapse.
**Weakest link:** The docs still spec the circular reward (value-through-rollout); the checker has zero implementation and one failed precedent; one realized future is a 1-sample draw from a stochastic branch; the reward scores forecasting, not explanation.

## 4. Changes to the Current Runbook

The ORDERED RUNBOOK ([1]–[7] in `claude-progress.txt`) **largely survives** — steps [1]–[3], [5] are unchanged, and [6] already contains the data-scaling curve and round-encoder retrain this review demands. Required changes:

**CHANGE A (fire now, documentation, ~1h) — Amend the Phase-3 reward spec.**
`docs/bridge-design.md` §5 and `docs/decisions-ledger.md` §2/§5 still name value-through-rollout as the sole GRPO quality signal. The red-team verdict is unambiguous: that reward is model-authored and uncorrectable without an environment; the grounded reward (verbalized predictions vs realized demo future) is the only non-circular option and currently lives only in a pivot header. Amend both docs; demote value-through-rollout to group *generator*. Any trainer built from the current docs silently implements the circular version. *(Weak-link #2.)*

**CHANGE B (amend [4], code) — The coverage harness must ship with its fair baseline and one new metric.**
Extend step [4] to include: (i) the E3 fair stochastic baseline (per-bucket damped-CV + fitted residual covariance, K=16, min-over-K scored identically) — without it, minADE-16 numbers from [6] are unfalsifiable against pure variance and cannot be reported; (ii) a trajectory-coherence metric (mode-switch rate of sampled depth-10/20 rollouts vs matched real trajectories) — it's the only instrument that can detect mode-dithering GRPO groups, and no current metric sees it. *(Weak-links #5, and the intent-token trigger.)*

**CHANGE C (amend [6], +hours of 4090, no pod cost) — Add the matched-capacity supervised ceiling.**
The round-encoder retrain alone gates the keystone on a legacy comparator. Add: same 19M architecture, same clean corpus, trained end-to-end on round_won (+ event heads), probed under the identical protocol. If it saturates, "best supervised effort" is finally established; if it doesn't, the label-starvation premise itself is falsified and better to know before the paper. *(Weak-link #9.)*

**CHANGE D (insert before the bridge ladder in [7], CPU/4090-cheap) — Three pre-QLoRA referees.**
Before any 35B pod spend: (i) grid-target capacity probe ([11,512] recon from head-output text vs from latent) — this single number decides soft-tokens vs head-readout; (ii) SAE/linear-probe audit for latent features not decodable from head outputs (same decision, causal form); (iii) bridge-tap layer sweep (layers 3–6, steerability + cross-frame persistence) — the final-norm tap is untested path-dependence and changing it after QLoRA is expensive. Also promote the head-Jacobian subspace from recon-fallback #2 to primary recon target — computable now from cached val frames. *(Weak-links #3, #4, #14; the lineage brief's top transfers.)*

**CHANGE E (amend [7] gate work, code) — Fix the faithfulness gate before it emits any number, and widen the conjunction.**
B1 (gold-leak into latent-off arm) and B3 (post-EOS bypass) invalidate both gate evals as written; B2 (value-agreement) should be implemented as the exact-match fact audit against tick ground truth (strictly subsumes it, near-free on templated text). Add the counterfactual flip-test (edit man-advantage/bomb/plant state, re-encode through the frozen WM, demand the text flips) as a co-equal causal leg — it targets the confabulation failure recon on a low-rank target cannot. *(Weak-link #4; NLA red-team verdict.)*

**CHANGE F (insert before GRPO in [7]) — Two feasibility gates and a sequencing swap.**
(i) Checker gate: build the text→claim extractor and require AUC ≥ ~0.75 on a constructed correct-vs-wrong pseudo-gold set — the only precedent (F08v4's matcher) is the documented failure; specify probabilistic scoring (CRPS/Brier), since a binary checker forfeits the calibration argument that justifies RL over hindsight-SFT. (ii) Reward-noise gate: ICC of checker scores under single-draw future noise on cached data; if <~0.2, group advantages are noise. (iii) Sequencing: run one offline ReST/rejection-sampling round (or margin-filtered DPO) with the validated checker before on-policy GRPO — it validates the whole reward stack, produces a stronger init, and needs no new infrastructure. *(Weak-link #16; GRPO verdict.)*

**CHANGE G (small edits to locked knobs' framing, not their content).**
(i) Knob 1's written rationale should be restated around the consumption argument (path-dependent value, joint coherence, per-step latent for the bridge) and the "direct heads can only learn the average" claim struck — it is false for anchor heads and a reviewer will catch it; add the cheap direct-head pilot at t+{40,80} as a control when the trunk exists. (ii) Knob 4's egocentric pilot trigger should be softened to horizons ≥2s (the current ALL-horizons form can never fire on partial evidence), and the two secondary holdout arms (~$20, pinned nuke contingency + one more) should be budgeted for publication rather than deferred to rebuttal. *(Weak-links #7, #13.)*
